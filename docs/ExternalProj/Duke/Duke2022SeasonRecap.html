Analysis of Duke Field Hockey’s 2022 Season

Author

Chase Mathis

Overview

Thanks to the vast amounts of data collected over the course of the season, the analytics team was able to combine the data and extract aggregate trends. In total, there are over 37504 recorded plays.

Location Frequency
Middle 127

The distribution is rather similar to Duke’s own distribution. However, opponents do a better job forcing us to the left. Duke is forced to the left 25.08 percent of the time, while opponents are forced to the left 18.15 percent of the time. On a better note, Duke entered the circle 64 more times than their opponents across the season.
